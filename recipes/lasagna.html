Lasagna

Delicious homemade lasagna

This lasagna is homemade, containing only fresh and tasty ingredients

You dont need a lot of skills to make this delicious dish, so lets start!

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomatoes
  • Ground beef
  • Parmezan cheese

Preparation

  1. Slightly bake the onions and garlic until slightly caramelized
  2. Toss in your ground beef and bake until golden brown, add some salt and pepper
  3. Add the tomatoes and some oregano
  4. Construct the lasagna with your ingredients starting with ground beef, cheese and a layer of lasagna sheets
